== Description ==

If you have been on a lookout for a powerful **WordPress translation plugin** that would allow your audiences to access your site's content in their preferred language, look no further: WP Translate is your best bet. This translation plugin uses the power of Google Translate to translate content into several different languages. Once you have successfully installed the WordPress Translation Plugin, your visitors will be able to access your website in different languages by simply selecting the preferred language option from the various options available.

There has never been a better time to be a blogger or website owner. Until a few years ago, bloggers and website owners faced an important dilemma: is it better to write in one's native language or a language that allows one to increase their reading base? This dilemma has finally been solved. WP Translate places a Google Translate widget on your website. This widget gives users the option of accessing your website's content in any language of their choice. By simply installing WP Translate, you will be able to make your blog or WordPress site multilingual.

= Benefits of Using the WordPress Translation Plugin =

WP Translate comes equipped with several handy features. Here are a few of them:

* This translation Plugin uses the power of Google automatic machine translation. Thus, the process of conversion is quick, and translations can be trusted for quality.
* This WordPress Translation Plugin supports Google Analytics Integration. Therefore, you will be able to get an in-depth idea of the visitors frequenting your blog or website.
* This plugin does not only translate your website or WordPress pages and posts, but it also effectively translates categories, tags, menus, and widgets.
* WP Translate also supports enhanced widget positioning.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is WP Translate? =
WP Translate is a **Google Language plugin** for WordPress.  Using the power of the Google Translate automatic language translation service, WP Translate gives your readers the ability to translate your website content into their own language.

= Why do I need a translation plugin? =
Our world is getting smaller and not having a multilingual site means you are getting left behind.  Many content providers spend hundreds to thousands of dollars having content translated.  WP Translate makes your content available to more than 99% of internet user by making your website multilingual.

When your site is available is multilingual, you become identified as a global brand. This attracts readers from the global market to your website or business.

= Will I be able to see what languages users are selecting? =
Yes.  WP Translate is one of the few WordPress translation plugins that integrates with Google Analytics.  Simply enter your site's UA code into the field provided in the settings section of the plugin. Your site's translation usage will be tracking in the 'Events' section of Google Analytics.

= How do I add Google Translate to WordPress? =
First, Download WP Translate from the WordPress plugin repository. Then, activate the plugin from your admin console.  It's as easy as that!  You now have a Google Translate tool bar your readers can use to translate your blog's content.  WP Translate also has a widget that will give you a drop-down list of languages your readers can use to translatre your content.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `wp-translate` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
